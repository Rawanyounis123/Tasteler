# Tasteler-Recipe App
Our App is a comprehensive mobile application designed to provide users with a seamless experience for discovering, saving, and exploring a wide variety of recipes. This app incorporates modern design principles, user-friendly navigation, and efficient data management to ensure a delightful journey for users.

![Recipe App](OurApp.png)

## Features

- **User Authentication and Registration**: Securely register and log in to your account.
- **Splash Screen**: Engaging Lottie animation to welcomes users.
- **Home Fragment**: Explore a curated list of recipes from the [MealDB API](https://www.themealdb.com/api.php).
- **Recipe Detail**: View recipe images, titles, and details. Save recipes as favorites.
- **Video Player**: Watch recipe videos using the built-in video player.
- **Favorite Recipes**: Keep track of your favorite recipes.
- **Search**: Search for recipes by name or keyword.
- **Bottom Navigation**: Effortlessly switch between Home, Favorites, Search, and more.
- **Options Menu**: Sign out or learn about the creator.
